About Plenar.io

Over the past few years, levels of government from the federal administration to individual municipalities like the City of Chicago have been moving toward an open data model, releasing their data publicly for free.

Plenar.io tells a unified story of place by centralizing data from these various sources in order to increase accessibility and help ordinary citizens and researchers draw connections between datasets. Data can be viewed via a map interface and accessed by download or via an API.
