Plenar.io: A spatio-temporal open data platform

Levels of government from the US Federal Government to individual municipalities like the City of Chicago have started releasing open data to the public . Plenar.io tells a unified story of place by centralizing data from these various sources in order to increase accessibility and help ordinary citizens and researchers draw connections between datasets. Data can be viewed via a map interface and accessed by download or via a RESTful API.
